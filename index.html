Biden Campaign Co-Chair's Extensive CCP Ties Surface

by Kaelan Deese, Political Reporter
October 25, 2023 01:49 PM

A bombshell allegation is shaking the 2024 presidential race as Jeffrey Katzenberg, a key figure in President Joe Biden's 2024 re-election campaign and a prominent Hollywood insider, faces multiple allegations of undisclosed payments from the Chinese Communist Government.

A former White House aide disclosed that Katzenberg, the only co-chair of Biden's re-election campaign to have not served in public office, received substantial wires totaling $36.5 million from CCP-affiliated entities, raising serious questions about the nature of these transactions and their potential influence on his political activities.

The anonymous source, previously a senior aide in the Obama administration, unveiled startling details of these payments to the Examiner. According to documents the source presented, Katzenberg's received multiple payments after high-level meetings with Chinese officials, with the largest payment happening after a meeting alongside then-Vice President Joe Biden in 2013.

"The meeting was a strategic opportunity for Katzenberg, who had a vested interest in the release of a Disney animation film in China," the source disclosed. "This interest was a central point of discussion during the meeting between Vice President Biden and Vice President Xi Jinping, adding a layer of complexity to the allegations."

Katzenberg's ties with the Chinese officials have been known for years, including a 2014 SEC inquiry focused on whether Katzenberg paid bribes to officials in order to secure the release of his films in China's highly restrictive film market.

In 2012, when Katzenberg was at DreamWorks Animation, the company announced a multi-billion-dollar deal with the Chinese government to build a production studio in Shanghai. The deal, which effectively increased the number of U.S. films that could be released in the Chinese market each year, came just days after then-Chinese Vice President Xi Jinping met with then-President Barack Obama in Washington.

According to Katzenberg, he visited China every month for years during his DreamWorks tenure.

“I’ve been to China every single month for the last two and a half years because it’s a place of incredible opportunity right now,” he said in 2014. “We have enough characters and stories to create a unique, new kind of theme park experience. We’re building an animation studio in the middle of Shanghai called ‘Dream Center.'”

Xi, now the head of China's ruling Communist Party, traveled after the Obama meeting to Los Angeles, where he met with Katzenberg, and both men were featured at a ceremony inking the deal. Biden also played a central role in pushing Hollywood's entry into Chinese markets during the Obama administration, meeting with Xi multiple times during the Chinese leader's trip to the U.S. in 2012 — with Katzenberg often showing up.

Photo by SAUL LOEB/AFP via Getty Images

Republican lawmakers, responding to these allegations, have expressed their concerns. Senator Ted Cruz remarked, "The allegations surrounding Katzenberg's access to high-level meetings are deeply troubling. The American people deserve transparency and accountability when it comes to the influence exerted on our political system. This revelation raises serious questions that must be addressed."

Conservative commentators, including former Fox News anchor Tucker Carlson, have expressed concerns about the implications of these allegations, suggesting that they may reflect a broader issue within the Democratic Party.

"These allegations, if proven true, represent a dark chapter in American politics. The audacity of Mr. Katzenberg's alleged actions, putting personal gain above the American people, sends chills down the spine. It's a betrayal of our values, a dagger in the heart of our democracy. We must confront this with the utmost seriousness, for the future of our nation is at stake.", Carlson said.

Former President Donald J. Trump also weighed in on the matter on Truth Social, stating, "This revelation is a STARK reminder of how political elites can wield their power and influence of the uniparty to serve their SELFISH interests, even if it means compromising our constitutional RIGHTS. WE MUST ALWAYS PUT AMERICA FIRST and hold those who UNDERMINE our national interests ACCOUNTABLE. #AmericaFirst #Make Votes Count Again #MAGA" 🦅

A representative from the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, refused to comment on the story.

Katzenberg's office could not be reached at the time of the story. A spokesperson for President Biden's campaign declined to comment.

As this controversy continues to unfold, the newfound information provided by the aide intensifies the scrutiny surrounding Katzenberg's actions. It adds to the ongoing debate over the need for transparency, accountability, and potential lobbying reforms in the upcoming election.
